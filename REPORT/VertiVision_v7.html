🦠

Disease Blindspot

Hydroponic chilly plants are vulnerable to Anthracnose, Leaf Curl, and Powdery Mildew — the three most destructive diseases in indoor chilly farming. Research confirms AI detects these diseases 2–3 days before the human eye can — the difference between intervention and a full crop write-off.

🧪

Nutrient Guesswork

Professional EC/pH meters cost ₹1,000–₹8,000 each and provide only a number — not an action. Calcium deficiency (the primary tip burn driver) causes irreversible cellular damage before any visual symptom appears.

🌐

Language Barrier

Every agri-advisory platform delivers content in English. Maharashtra's farming community communicates in Marathi and Hindi. GSMA analysis found this single gap is the primary reason Plantix adoption rates among small farmers remain low (GSMA, 2021).

How It Works — 5 Steps

  • 01Phone on ₹100 tripod, pointed at Chilly tower. Camera auto-activates at 6 AM, 12 PM and 6 PM — fully autonomous. Farmer does nothing.
  • 02YOLOv8n-cls analyses each Chilly plant tier. Anthracnose, Leaf Curl, Powdery Mildew + healthy classes. LED domain-adapted model — not PlantVillage.
  • 03EfficientNet-B0 classifies growth stage simultaneously. Predicts days to harvest. Grad-CAM heatmap shows exactly which region triggered the result.
  • 04Disease/anomaly → Claude Haiku API generates hydroponic-specific advice in under 2 seconds. Correct nutrient dosage. Immediate action.
  • 05WhatsApp report in Marathi / Hindi / English with annotated image. No app download. No login. Push notification to farmer's phone.

Why WhatsApp

500M+ Indian users. No install. Works on any phone. Message history = treatment log. ET Edge Insights: WhatsApp advisory showed measurably higher farmer engagement than every other digital channel tested.

Grad-CAM XAI

Every diagnosis includes a heatmap — red region = where AI focused. Farmer sees exactly which leaf area triggered the alert. Trustworthy AI, not a black box. Research standard in medical AI.
